About Me

I'm Ashik Paul, a UI/UX designer and frontend developer based in Kerala, India. I specialise in turning ideas into clean, functional digital products that people actually enjoy using.

I care deeply about design details — typography, spacing, colour, and how everything comes together. At the same time I make sure every project I build is accessible, fast, and semantically correct.

When I'm not designing or coding, I'm probably sketching ideas, exploring design trends, or experimenting with new CSS techniques.
